Speeds up Research

We did the hard yards to provide you with a one stop shop of services, educational resources, guides and policies offered by the University of Auckland. Simply select the type of offering you are after or use the search bar at the top of the page to find offerings relevant to your research!

school

Researcher Focused

The Research Hub has been organised to allow you to search and filter research offerings through a number of different categories, including research lifecycle stage, discipline area, service type, study level and policy area. This enables you to quickly find offerings that are relevant to your research needs.

list

Details

Each offering is described in detail, including the features, limitations, considerations, eligibility requirements, cost and where to find support. Each offering has a section with reviews written by fellow researchers, write a review to help other researchers if you are feeling generous!
